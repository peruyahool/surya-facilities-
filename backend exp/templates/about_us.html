A Versatile and Strategic Entrepreneur in Service Industry.

About Shri P Ravi- Chairman of SRM Groups

Shri. P. Ravi is the Chairman of SRM Groups, one of the most reputed education institutions in India. Born on 15th March 1967, he is the son of Dr. T. R. Pachamuthu, Founder and Chancellor of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, an Educationist, a Philanthropist and an Industrialist. His mother Smt.P. Easwari is an Educationist and Treasurer of Valliammai Society, constituted with the objective for establishing educational institutions. A Post Graduate in Literature, Shri. P. Ravi holds responsibility for SRM Groups’ Corporate Functions, including Finance, Strategy Management, Business Plan, Optimization of Resources, and Innovations.He is also a renowned edupreneur, industrialist, and a philanthropist. Mr. Ravi Pachamoothoo is an ardent traveler and enjoys traveling across the world. Traveling provides him an opportunity to experience the working nuances of industries and enterprises which could be incorporated into the Group’s activities and set them apart as leaders in the relevant fields.

An Entrepreneur

As Dr. T. R. Pachamoothoo laid the strong foundation for the SRM Group, Mr. Ravi Pachamoothoo has further expanded the frontiers of the SRM group from an educational institute to a business empire with the largest portfolio of institutions in South India. For the past 30 years, he has been actively associated in setting up of several educational institutions under SRM Groups. The SRM Educational Group as a community has over 80,000 students and 4,500 staff spreading over nine campuses in India now. The SRM Group has earned the best teaching quality rating and a strong research record under the leadership of Shri. P. Ravi. The institution offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering & Technology, Management Studies, Medicine and Health Sciences, Law, and Science & Humanities.

A Philanthropist

As a philanthropist, Shri Ravi has developed SRM hospitals at Kattankulathur, Chengalpattu, Chennai with 1225 beds and state of the Art medical equipment to ensure that the people living in the surrounding remote areas of lower economic strata of the society are treated. He has also started a 750 bedded state of the art hospital in Trichy to cater to the needs of the rural population. He has initiated several scholarships for meritorious and economically challenged students to enable them to achieve higher levels and goals in life. He provides books, uniforms and tuition fees to the children belonging to economically backward section. Mr. Ravi was one of the first to contribute to the Chief Minister’s Relief Fund for the benefit of people affected by the Cyclone Thane in 2013.He has also contributed to the “Prime Minister’s” Relief Fund for flood victims in Kashmir. SRM, under his chairmanship, has decided to provide full scholarship (tuition fees and hostel fees) to the students hailing from the Thane cyclone affected areas and belonging to the affected agricultural families for pursuing their engineering college till the final year.SRM group has also contributed liberally to the development of under developed districts in the stare through the collector’s welfare scheme. Besides, Shri.P. Ravi has been contributing to renovate the various dilapidated places of worship in remote villages to retain the age-old traditions and practices carried out there.He provides funds to the needy for taking medical assistance from the hospitals.

AN INDUSTRIALIST

Mr. Ravi Pachamoothoo rise as an industrialist is well commemorated in the establishment of SIMS hospitals, hotels, cements, civil works, auto-tech, Trac Media and transportation.

SIMS Hospital

Established in 2012, the SIMS Hospital in Chennai renders 360-degree advanced tertiary healthcare services with multi-super specialty and a state-of-the-art multi-organ transplant service. With the finest combination of experience, expertise, state-of-the-art technology and well-coordinated patient-centric teamwork, the hospital aims to provide utmost care to its patients and ensures excellence in treatment. Following the footsteps of the SRM Group- a large conglomerate with a leading presence in education, medical, transport and infotainment, the SIMS Hospital strives to establish trust and excellence. Its vision is to offer an advanced full range of primary and specialty care medical care services as well as enabling cross specialty consultation for unmatched patient experience. Shri. P. Ravi is instrumental in the growth and expansion of the SIMS Hospitals across Chennai. More than 1500 Employees were employed across all the hospitals under his umbrella.

Hotels

Shri Ravi has entered into the hotel industry in the year 1995 and his passion for the Hospitality Industry has enabled the Group to set up six hotels of different categories at various locations in Tamil Nadu. He lays emphasis on quality, dedicated service, and focus on delivering sustainable growth. The Hotels under his control are acclaimed and conferred with awards. More than 750 Employees were employed across all the hotels.

Cements

Shri Ravi has established Hemadri Cements Ltd., at Jaggayyapet in Andhra Pradesh. The plant was commissioned in March 1985. Mr. Pachamoothoo has a Midas’ touch in whatever ventures he turns his attention to. He took over a sick cement company situated in the state of Andhra Pradesh and within a short span of time, he turned it into a profit-making venture. The company provides direct employment to over 500 people and indirect employment to many. The cement unit has now initiated steps for expansion of its capacity. The manufactured cements surpass all the stringent quality Control parameters set by the Bureau of Indian Standards (B.I. S) and the Research & Consultancy Directorate. Both Hemadri 43 Grade and Hemadri Gold 53 Grade O.P.C are manufactured using Vedadri Lime Stone, renowned to be one of the best lime stones available in the country.

Civil Works

Shri Ravi has set his foot in the SRM Civil Works Private Limited in the year 1998, which is yet another company, engaged in the production of ready-mix concretes used for construction activities and others. He has also got two factories located in the suburbs of Chennai.

SRM Auto- Tech

SRM Auto- Tech is another renowned establishment started by Shri. P. Ravi. Established in the year 2013 located at kandigai, Chennai, SRM Auto Tech now has more than 100 employees at the unit. Bus building is an amalgamation of art and science, with the primary focus on contemporary designs and robust structure. SRM AutoTech manufactures state-of-art, technologically enabled buses at affordable prices. With the wide range of available bus bodies, our customers are never short of choices and we have 1200+ satisfied customers. Abiding by budget limits and sticking to deadlines have been our forte since inception. In the year 2014, he started a media company called Trac Media Private Limited which runs as a successful general entertainment TV channel in the name of Vendhar TV. It telecasts regular news updates and entertainment. Shri Ravi has started SRM Transports Private Limited in the year 1999, which has a fleet of over 120 buses plying across the five southern states and about 30 car carriers, minibuses, bulkers, Speed Parcel Carriers and tourist cabs. More than 50 employees are employed in the unit.
